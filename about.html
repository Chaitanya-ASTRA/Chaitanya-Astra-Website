ONE TEAM, DIVERSE MISSIONS

Sky is no longer the limit, Space or nothing, Universe it is.

Our Mission at Chaitanya Astra is to educate students on the importance of rocketry, missiles, systems engineering, aerospace technologies, and mechatronic systems. We aim to equip them with the skills needed to succeed in real-world aerospace work, while exploring next-generation space technologies in a student-driven context.

Chaitanya Astra provides students the chance to apply classroom knowledge to projects with tangible challenges and results. As a fast-growing student organization, we offer hands-on experience in designing, building, and flying various aerospace vehicles and mechatronic systems, fostering an environment of interdisciplinary ideas and diverse levels of experience.
